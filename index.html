What is Geomundus?

Geomundus is an international symposium held by the students from the consortium of Erasmus Mundus (EM) International Master's program in Geospatial Technologies. It is a free conference organized by students, for students. This year (2016) the conference will be held in Castellón de la Plana, Spain from 4th to 5st of November.
